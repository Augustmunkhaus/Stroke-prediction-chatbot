# Stroke Prediction Chatbot
A machine learning chatbot that predicts your risk of stroke based on personal health data.

## What It Does
Train and evaluate multiple ML models on a stroke dataset, then chat with the best-performing model through a conversational interface. You provide your health details naturally in the chat, and the model returns your stroke risk as a percentage.

Collected inputs include age, BMI, glucose level, hypertension, heart disease, smoking status, and more.

## Tech Stack
- **ML:** scikit-learn (Logistic Regression, Random Forest, artificial neural network)
- **UI:** Gradio
- **Chatbot:** Ollama LLM
- **Other:** NLTK, pandas, numpy
